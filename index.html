Information Warfare (IW) & Electronic Warfare (EW)

Information Warfare (IW)

Information Warfare focuses on controlling and exploiting information to gain an advantage in decision-making. It spans cyber operations, psychological operations, military deception, and strategic communications.

  • Cyber Operations: Attacks and defense on digital networks.
  • Psychological Operations: Shaping perceptions and behavior.
  • Military Deception & OPSEC: Misleading adversaries, protecting intent.
  • Strategic Communications: Influencing the infosphere with narratives.

Electronic Warfare (EW)

Electronic Warfare uses the electromagnetic spectrum to disrupt enemy systems while protecting friendly use. It includes sensing, attacking, and protecting within the RF domain.

  • Electronic Support (ES): Detecting and analyzing enemy signals.
  • Electronic Attack (EA): Jamming, spoofing, directed energy.
  • Electronic Protection (EP): Safeguarding friendly communications and sensors.
  • Spectrum Management: Coordinating spectrum use in operations.
